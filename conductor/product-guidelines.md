# Product Guidelines

## Communication Tone
- **Friendly & Welcoming:** Use supportive, clear, and non-technical language. The goal is to make the user feel confident and guided, especially if they are not tech-savvy. Avoid jargon where possible.

## Visual Identity & UI
- **Clean & Modern:** The interface should prioritize clarity and ease of use.
    - Use high contrast and ample whitespace to reduce visual clutter.
    - Buttons and primary actions should be large and clearly labeled.
    - Focus on a modern aesthetic that facilitates a "zero-config" workflow.

## Interaction & Error Handling
- **Gentle Guidance:**
    - Background tasks should be communicated subtly, ensuring the user knows the app is working without being overwhelmed by data.
    - When errors occur, use human-readable explanations instead of raw error codes.
    - Always provide a clear path forward (e.g., "Try Again," "Check Connection," or "Contact Support") to keep the experience seamless.
